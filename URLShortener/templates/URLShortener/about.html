URL Shortener

  1. Easy substitute of a long URL up to 600 characters. Submit an URL and recieve an alias which will
  2. redirect you to your URL. For every entry a usert is picked randomly from users available in the database.
  3. Using a '!' between the domain and your short URL will direct you the site with the details about the user assigned and direct URL e.g.:
    url with redirection: https://ten-clouds-interview.herokuapp.com/ABCD
    url with direct URL details: https://ten-clouds-interview.herokuapp.com/!ABCD
